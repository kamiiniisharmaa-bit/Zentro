About Zentro

We believe that the spaces we inhabit should be a reflection of who we are. Zentro was born from a passion for timeless design, quality craftsmanship, and the simple joy of a well-made object.

Founded in 2020 · San Francisco, CA

Our Mission

To curate and create home essentials that blend modern aesthetics with enduring quality. We are dedicated to making design accessible, sustainable, and personal — because your home should feel as unique as you are.

What We Value

Integrity

Honest sourcing, transparent pricing, and ethical partnerships.

Sustainability

Mindful materials, reduced waste, and a commitment to a better planet.

Craftsmanship

Every piece is thoughtfully designed and meticulously made to last.

Community

We celebrate the people who bring our vision to life — from makers to customers.

Our Story

Zentro began in a small studio with a simple idea: that the things we surround ourselves with should bring us joy every day. What started as a passion project quickly grew into a curated destination for modern home goods, loved by design enthusiasts and minimalists alike.

Today, we collaborate with artisans and designers from around the world, blending traditional techniques with contemporary forms. From furniture to décor, every Zentro piece tells a story of quality, intention, and love for the craft.
